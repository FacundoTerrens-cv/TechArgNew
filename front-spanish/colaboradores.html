SalCir is put up in a star network, where gateways are placed out to create coverage area. Each gateway have a reach of a 1000 meters, and detects all SalCir-chips within the area. You can have unlimited amount of SalCir-chips communicating in real time, and the data can be sent to an unlimited amount of applications. It's possible to configure the network like mesh, or a combination of mesh and star network.
